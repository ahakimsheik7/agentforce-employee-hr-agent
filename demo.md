# Demo — Agentforce Employee HR Assistant Agent

## Overview

This document demonstrates how the **Employee HR Assistant Agent built with Salesforce Agentforce** responds to common employee questions.

The goal of the AI agent is to help employees quickly access HR-related information such as benefits, PTO policies, and HR contact information.

The following examples illustrate typical interactions between employees and the AI agent.

---

# Demo Scenario 1 — Employee Benefits Question

### Employee Question

What health insurance benefits does the company offer?

### Agent Processing

Topic identified:
Employee Benefits

Action triggered:
Search HR Knowledge Base

### Agent Response

Our company offers several healthcare benefits including medical, dental, and vision coverage.

Employees can choose from multiple health insurance plans depending on their needs. For detailed information about plan options and coverage, please review the Employee Benefits Guide in the HR knowledge base.

---

# Demo Scenario 2 — PTO Policy Question

### Employee Question

How many PTO days do employees receive each year?

### Agent Processing

Topic identified:
PTO Policy

Action triggered:
Retrieve PTO Policy Documentation

### Agent Response

Employees receive paid time off based on their years of service with the company.

New employees receive a standard PTO allocation each year, which increases over time. For full details about vacation, sick leave, and holiday policies, please review the PTO Policy documentation available through the HR portal.

---

# Demo Scenario 3 — HR Contact Request

### Employee Question

How can I contact HR?

### Agent Processing

Topic identified:
HR Contact

Action triggered:
Provide HR Contact Information

### Agent Response

You can contact the Human Resources department by email or through the internal HR support portal.

For general HR questions, please email the HR support team or submit a request through the employee help desk system.

---

# Demo Scenario 4 — HR Process Question

### Employee Question

How do I request time off?

### Agent Processing

Topic identified:
PTO Policy

Action triggered:
Retrieve HR Process Documentation

### Agent Response

To request time off, log into the employee portal and submit a PTO request through the time management system.

Your manager will review and approve the request based on availability and team scheduling.

For additional guidance, please review the PTO request process documentation.

---
